## Oahu (May 27 night → May 31 afternoon) — no car

**Why no car:** [Waikiki](https://www.google.com/search?q=Waikiki+Honolulu) parking runs $35–50/night and TheBus + walking covers everything. Get a [HOLO card](https://www.google.com/search?q=HOLO+card+Oahu) at any 7-Eleven: $3/ride, $7.50/day cap, $30/week cap.

### Lodging
All 4 in one room — filter for **2 queen beds** (queens are bigger than fulls, and most Waikiki hotels list rooms this way). Target ~$200–280/night for the whole room.

- **[Holiday Inn Express Waikiki](https://www.google.com/search?q=Holiday+Inn+Express+Waikiki)** — 2-queen rooms, free breakfast, 5-min walk to beach.
- **[Aqua Aloha Surf Waikiki](https://www.google.com/search?q=Aqua+Aloha+Surf+Waikiki)** — 2-queen rooms, kitchenette in some, often $200ish.
- **[Coconut Waikiki Hotel](https://www.google.com/search?q=Coconut+Waikiki+Hotel)** — 2-queen rooms, retro vibe, walkable to everything.
- **[Ohana Waikiki East by Outrigger](https://www.google.com/search?q=Ohana+Waikiki+East+by+Outrigger)** — reliable, 2-queen rooms.
- Cheapest fallback: **[Polynesian Hostel Beach Club](https://www.google.com/search?q=Polynesian+Hostel+Beach+Club+Waikiki)** private quad room (4 separate twins, not 2 fulls — only if hotel rates spike).
- Airbnb/VRBO works too — filter for "2 beds, sleeps 4" in Waikiki or [Ala Moana](https://www.google.com/search?q=Ala+Moana+Honolulu).

### May 27 (arrival, late)
- Land 11:57 PM. Skip TheBus (late runs sparse). Split a Lyft/Uber to Waikiki (~$35–45 for 4).
- Crash.

### May 28 — Diamond Head + Waikiki
- **Sunrise [Diamond Head](https://www.google.com/search?q=Diamond+Head+Oahu) hike** ($5/person, reservation REQUIRED at gostateparks.hawaii.gov — book NOW).
- Breakfast at **[Diamond Head Market & Grill](https://www.google.com/search?q=Diamond+Head+Market+and+Grill)** or cheap loco moco at [Rainbow Drive-In](https://www.google.com/search?q=Rainbow+Drive-In+Honolulu).
- Afternoon: [Waikiki Beach](https://www.google.com/search?q=Waikiki+Beach), free hula at [Kuhio Beach](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kuhio+Beach+hula+show) (check schedule).
- Dinner: **[Marukame Udon](https://www.google.com/search?q=Marukame+Udon+Waikiki)** (~$8/person, line moves fast).

### May 29 — Pearl Harbor + North Shore (long day)
- Morning: **[USS Arizona Memorial](https://www.google.com/search?q=USS+Arizona+Memorial)** (free; $1 reservation fee on recreation.gov — book 8 weeks ahead, otherwise try same-day walk-up at 7 AM).
- TheBus Route 20 from Waikiki → [Pearl Harbor](https://www.google.com/search?q=Pearl+Harbor+Hawaii).
- Afternoon: Bus 52 to **[North Shore](https://www.google.com/search?q=North+Shore+Oahu)** (~2 hr, but it's the cheap way). Eat at **[Giovanni's Shrimp Truck](https://www.google.com/search?q=Giovanni%27s+Shrimp+Truck+Haleiwa)** ($15) in [Haleiwa](https://www.google.com/search?q=Haleiwa+Oahu), swim at [Waimea Bay](https://www.google.com/search?q=Waimea+Bay+Oahu).
- Sunset bus back. Long day but free/cheap.

### May 30 — Snorkel + East side
- **[Hanauma Bay](https://www.google.com/search?q=Hanauma+Bay+Nature+Preserve)** ($25/person, reservations open 48 hrs ahead at 7 AM HST sharp — set an alarm, slots vanish in seconds). Best snorkeling on Oahu. Bring own gear or rent on-site ($20).
- TheBus Route 22 from Waikiki direct.
- Or alternate (free): **[Lanikai Pillbox](https://www.google.com/search?q=Lanikai+Pillbox+hike)** hike + [Lanikai](https://www.google.com/search?q=Lanikai+Beach)/[Kailua Beach](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kailua+Beach+Park+Oahu) (Bus 67).
- Dinner: **[Rainbow Drive-In](https://www.google.com/search?q=Rainbow+Drive-In+Honolulu)** plate lunch.

### May 31 — light morning, fly out
- Walk Waikiki Beach, grab [Leonard's malasadas](https://www.google.com/search?q=Leonard%27s+Bakery+malasadas+Honolulu).
- Lyft to HNL by 12:30 PM. Flight 2:40 PM.

## Big Island (May 31 evening → June 4 night)

### Rental Car
- **Book NOW** — Big Island prices spike close to date. Compare:
  - **[Costco Travel](https://www.google.com/search?q=Costco+Travel+rental+car+Hawaii)** (best rates if anyone has membership)
  - **[Discount Hawaii Car Rental](https://www.google.com/search?q=Discount+Hawaii+Car+Rental)** (aggregator, often beats direct)
  - **[Turo](https://www.google.com/search?q=Turo+Kona+Big+Island)** as backup
- Get an SUV or midsize — [saddle road](https://www.google.com/search?q=Saddle+Road+Big+Island+Hawaii) and Volcano park have rough patches but no 4WD needed for paved areas. Skip the 4WD upsell unless going to Mauna Kea summit.
- Pickup at KOA on arrival, return before 9:25 PM flight on 6/4.

### Lodging
All 4 in one room with 2 queen beds. Hotel options run ~$180–250/night for the whole room; Airbnbs around the same with a kitchen, which is the budget-saver.

- **[Kona Seaside Hotel](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kona+Seaside+Hotel)** — cheapest reliable option in [Kailua-Kona](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kailua-Kona+Hawaii), 2-queen rooms, walkable to seawall.
- **[Royal Kona Resort](https://www.google.com/search?q=Royal+Kona+Resort)** — 2-queen oceanview rooms, often $230ish, big pool.
- **[Holiday Inn Express Kailua-Kona](https://www.google.com/search?q=Holiday+Inn+Express+Kailua-Kona)** — 2-queen rooms, free breakfast.
- **Airbnb/VRBO** in Kailua-Kona, [Holualoa](https://www.google.com/search?q=Holualoa+Hawaii), or [Captain Cook](https://www.google.com/search?q=Captain+Cook+Hawaii+town) — filter "2 beds, sleeps 4" with kitchen. Best budget pick because you can cook.
- **Recommended split**: 3 nights Kona + 1 night near Volcano village (5/31, 6/2, 6/3 in Kona; 6/1 near Volcano after the park day). Saves a brutal 5-hour round-trip drive on Volcanoes day. Look at **[Volcano Village Lodge](https://www.google.com/search?q=Volcano+Village+Lodge)**, **[Kīlauea Lodge](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kilauea+Lodge+Volcano+Hawaii)**, or any [Volcano Village](https://www.google.com/search?q=Volcano+Village+Hawaii) Airbnb (~$150/night).

### Food/Money savers
- **[Costco Kona](https://www.google.com/search?q=Costco+Kona)** (membership required) — gas is cheapest on island, plus stock up on poke, rotisserie chicken, water.
- **[Walmart Kona](https://www.google.com/search?q=Walmart+Kailua-Kona)** for groceries if no Costco.
- Cook breakfast + pack lunch from Airbnb. Eat out 1 meal/day.

### May 31 — arrival
- Land 3:29 PM, pick up car, hit Costco/Walmart for groceries + gas.
- Sunset at **[Magic Sands Beach](https://www.google.com/search?q=Magic+Sands+Beach+Kona)** or **[Kahaluʻu Beach](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kahaluu+Beach+Park+Kona)** (turtles).
- Easy dinner at Airbnb.

### June 1 — Hawaii Volcanoes National Park (full day)
- Leave Kona ~7 AM, ~2.5 hr drive via Hwy 11 (south route, more scenic).
- **[Hawaii Volcanoes National Park](https://www.google.com/search?q=Hawaii+Volcanoes+National+Park) entry $30/vehicle, valid 7 days.**
- Stops: [Kīlauea Visitor Center](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kilauea+Visitor+Center) → [Crater Rim Drive](https://www.google.com/search?q=Crater+Rim+Drive+Hawaii) → [Steam Vents](https://www.google.com/search?q=Steam+Vents+Hawaii+Volcanoes) → [Kīlauea Iki overlook](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kilauea+Iki+overlook) → **[Thurston Lava Tube](https://www.google.com/search?q=Thurston+Lava+Tube)** → [Chain of Craters Road](https://www.google.com/search?q=Chain+of+Craters+Road) down to the [Hōlei Sea Arch](https://www.google.com/search?q=Holei+Sea+Arch).
- Pack lunch, fill gas in Volcano village.
- Stay past dark to see crater glow if [Kīlauea](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kilauea+volcano+current+activity) is erupting (check nps.gov/havo for current activity).
- En route back: **[Punaluʻu Black Sand Beach](https://www.google.com/search?q=Punaluu+Black+Sand+Beach)** (sea turtles, often) if daylight, or save for next day.

### June 2 — Hilo side waterfalls + black sand
- Drive [Saddle Road (Hwy 200)](https://www.google.com/search?q=Saddle+Road+Hwy+200+Hawaii) across to [Hilo](https://www.google.com/search?q=Hilo+Hawaii) (~1.5 hr).
- **[Rainbow Falls](https://www.google.com/search?q=Rainbow+Falls+Hilo)** (free, 5 min from parking).
- **[Akaka Falls State Park](https://www.google.com/search?q=Akaka+Falls+State+Park)** ($5/vehicle, 442-ft falls, easy loop).
- Lunch in Hilo: **[Suisan Fish Market](https://www.google.com/search?q=Suisan+Fish+Market+Hilo)** poke bowls (~$12).
- **[Punaluʻu Black Sand Beach](https://www.google.com/search?q=Punaluu+Black+Sand+Beach)** on the way back via south route, or return via saddle.

### June 3 — Kona snorkel day
- **[Two Step (Honaunau Bay)](https://www.google.com/search?q=Two+Step+Honaunau+Bay+snorkeling)** — best shore snorkeling on island, free. Get there by 8 AM for parking + calm water.
- **[Puʻuhonua o Hōnaunau](https://www.google.com/search?q=Puuhonua+o+Honaunau+National+Historical+Park)** (Place of Refuge) next door, $20/vehicle.
- Afternoon: **[Kealakekua Bay](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kealakekua+Bay+Captain+Cook+Monument)** lookout, or chill at Magic Sands.
- Sunset dinner: split a fish plate at **[Da Poke Shack](https://www.google.com/search?q=Da+Poke+Shack+Kona)** or grab from Costco.

### June 4 — last day, late flight
- Morning: **[Hapuna Beach](https://www.google.com/search?q=Hapuna+Beach+State+Park)** (one of best beaches in US, free, north of Kona ~45 min).
- OR **[Mauna Kea Visitor Information Station](https://www.google.com/search?q=Mauna+Kea+Visitor+Information+Station)** at 9,200 ft — free, sunset is incredible. Acclimatize 30 min. Don't drive to summit without 4WD/permit; the visitor center is enough.
- Return rental by ~7 PM, dinner at airport-area food, fly 9:25 PM.

### What the buffer buys (pick one or split)
- **[Doors-off helicopter over Kīlauea](https://www.google.com/search?q=doors+off+helicopter+tour+Kilauea+Big+Island)** — ~$300/person (a stretch, but unforgettable if active)
- **[Manta ray night snorkel from Kona](https://www.google.com/search?q=manta+ray+night+snorkel+Kona)** — ~$120/person, world-class
- **[Pearl Harbor Battleship Missouri tour](https://www.google.com/search?q=Battleship+Missouri+Memorial+Pearl+Harbor)** — ~$35/person on top of free Arizona Memorial
- **[Sunset catamaran out of Waikiki](https://www.google.com/search?q=Waikiki+sunset+catamaran+sail)** — ~$60/person with drinks
- **[Atlantis Submarine Waikiki](https://www.google.com/search?q=Atlantis+Submarine+Waikiki)** — ~$120/person (skip — overrated)
- **[Luau](https://www.google.com/search?q=best+budget+luau+Oahu)** — ~$150/person (Old Lahaina–style on Oahu; skip on Big Island)
- **Nicer dinner** once on each island — ~$60/person each

### Where to upgrade if comfort > savings
- **Oahu lodging**: bump to ~$300/night (~$75/person/night) for a Waikiki condo with kitchen + parking — makes the late arrival much easier.
- **Big Island lodging**: split between Kona (3 nights) and Volcano village (1 night near the park) so the Volcanoes day isn't a 5-hour round-trip drive. Volcano cabins/Airbnbs run ~$150/night.
- **Rental car**: pay for the SUV upgrade (~$15/day extra) — much more comfortable for 4 + luggage on the long drives.
