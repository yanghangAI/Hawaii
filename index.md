## Oahu (May 27 night → May 31 afternoon) — no rental car

**Why no car:** [Waikiki](https://www.google.com/search?q=Waikiki+Honolulu) parking is $35–50/night. [TheBus](https://www.google.com/search?q=TheBus+Oahu+HOLO+card) + Lyft + booked tours cover everything. Get a [HOLO card](https://www.google.com/search?q=HOLO+card+Oahu) at any 7-Eleven: $3/ride, $7.50/day cap, $30/week cap.

### Lodging
All 4 in one room — filter for **2 queen beds**. Target ~$200–280/night for the whole room.

- **[Holiday Inn Express Waikiki](https://www.google.com/search?q=Holiday+Inn+Express+Waikiki)** — 2-queen rooms, free breakfast, 5-min walk to beach.
- **[Aqua Aloha Surf Waikiki](https://www.google.com/search?q=Aqua+Aloha+Surf+Waikiki)** — 2-queen rooms, kitchenette in some, often $200ish.
- **[Coconut Waikiki Hotel](https://www.google.com/search?q=Coconut+Waikiki+Hotel)** — 2-queen rooms, retro vibe, walkable to everything.
- **[Ohana Waikiki East by Outrigger](https://www.google.com/search?q=Ohana+Waikiki+East+by+Outrigger)** — reliable, 2-queen rooms.
- Airbnb/VRBO works too — filter for "2 beds, sleeps 4" in Waikiki or [Ala Moana](https://www.google.com/search?q=Ala+Moana+Honolulu).

### May 28 (Thu) — Snorkel + Diamond Head
- **Morning: snorkel at [Sans Souci / Kaimana Beach](https://www.google.com/search?q=Sans+Souci+Beach+snorkeling)** (free, walk from Waikiki, calm reef, no reservation needed).
  - Backup if you scored a reservation: **[Hanauma Bay](https://www.google.com/search?q=Hanauma+Bay+Nature+Preserve)** ($25/person, much better visibility — see booking notes below).

- **3:00 PM: [Diamond Head](https://www.google.com/search?q=Diamond+Head+Oahu) hike** ($5/person, reservation required at gostateparks.hawaii.gov).

### May 29 (Fri) — Kualoa Ranch + Lanikai (Circle Island tour)

- **Alternative**: book Kualoa Movie Sites tour only (~$120/person) and skip Lanikai — saves $30/person but loses the beach.

## Big Island (May 31 evening → June 4 night)

### Rental Car
- **Book NOW** — Big Island prices spike close to date. Compare:
  - **[Costco Travel](https://www.google.com/search?q=Costco+Travel+rental+car+Hawaii)** (best rates if anyone has membership)
  - **[Discount Hawaii Car Rental](https://www.google.com/search?q=Discount+Hawaii+Car+Rental)** (aggregator, often beats direct)
  - **[Turo](https://www.google.com/search?q=Turo+Kona+Big+Island)** as backup
- Get an SUV or midsize. Skip 4WD upsell unless going to Mauna Kea summit.
- Pickup at KOA on arrival, return before 9:25 PM flight on 6/4.

### Lodging
All 4 in one room with 2 queen beds. Hotel ~$180–250/night; Airbnbs around the same with a kitchen (the budget-saver — cook your own breakfast/lunch).

- **[Kona Seaside Hotel](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kona+Seaside+Hotel)** — cheapest reliable option in [Kailua-Kona](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kailua-Kona+Hawaii), 2-queen rooms.
- **[Royal Kona Resort](https://www.google.com/search?q=Royal+Kona+Resort)** — 2-queen oceanview, ~$230, big pool.
- **[Holiday Inn Express Kailua-Kona](https://www.google.com/search?q=Holiday+Inn+Express+Kailua-Kona)** — 2-queen rooms, free breakfast.
- **Airbnb/VRBO** in Kailua-Kona, [Holualoa](https://www.google.com/search?q=Holualoa+Hawaii), or [Captain Cook](https://www.google.com/search?q=Captain+Cook+Hawaii+town) — filter "2 beds, sleeps 4" with kitchen.
- **Recommended split**: 3 nights Kona + 1 night near [Volcano Village](https://www.google.com/search?q=Volcano+Village+Hawaii) (5/31, 6/2, 6/3 in Kona; 6/1 near Volcano after the park day). Saves a brutal 5-hour round-trip drive on Volcanoes day. Look at **[Volcano Village Lodge](https://www.google.com/search?q=Volcano+Village+Lodge)** or **[Kīlauea Lodge](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kilauea+Lodge+Volcano+Hawaii)** (~$150/night).

### Food strategy (no restaurant recs)
- **[Costco Kona](https://www.google.com/search?q=Costco+Kona)** (membership required) — cheapest gas on island; stock up on poke, rotisserie chicken, fruit, water.
- **[Walmart Kona](https://www.google.com/search?q=Walmart+Kailua-Kona)** if no Costco access.
- Cook breakfast + pack lunch from the Airbnb. Eat out 1 meal/day max.

### June 1 — Hawaii Volcanoes National Park
- Leave Kona ~7 AM, ~2.5 hr drive via Hwy 11 (south route, more scenic).
- **[Hawaii Volcanoes National Park](https://www.google.com/search?q=Hawaii+Volcanoes+National+Park) entry $30/vehicle, valid 7 days.**
- Stops: [Kīlauea Visitor Center](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kilauea+Visitor+Center) → [Crater Rim Drive](https://www.google.com/search?q=Crater+Rim+Drive+Hawaii) → [Steam Vents](https://www.google.com/search?q=Steam+Vents+Hawaii+Volcanoes) → [Kīlauea Iki overlook](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kilauea+Iki+overlook) → **[Thurston Lava Tube](https://www.google.com/search?q=Thurston+Lava+Tube)** → [Chain of Craters Road](https://www.google.com/search?q=Chain+of+Craters+Road) down to [Hōlei Sea Arch](https://www.google.com/search?q=Holei+Sea+Arch).
- Pack lunch, fill gas in Volcano village.
- Stay past dark to see crater glow if [Kīlauea](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kilauea+volcano+current+activity) is erupting (check nps.gov/havo).

### June 2 — Hilo waterfalls + Mauna Kea night
- Drive [Saddle Road (Hwy 200)](https://www.google.com/search?q=Saddle+Road+Hwy+200+Hawaii) to [Hilo](https://www.google.com/search?q=Hilo+Hawaii) (~1.5 hr).
- **[Rainbow Falls](https://www.google.com/search?q=Rainbow+Falls+Hilo)** (free, 5 min from parking).
- **[Akaka Falls State Park](https://www.google.com/search?q=Akaka+Falls+State+Park)** ($5/vehicle, 442-ft falls, easy loop).
- Late afternoon: drive back via Saddle Road to **[Mauna Kea Visitor Information Station](https://www.google.com/search?q=Mauna+Kea+Visitor+Information+Station)** at 9,200 ft (~1.5 hr from Akaka).
- **Sunset 云海 (sea of clouds)** — VIS sits above the trade-wind cloud deck, the cloud layer turns gold/pink at sunset (~6:55 PM in early June).
- **银河 (Milky Way) stargazing** — VIS is in one of the darkest skies on Earth, no telescopes needed for naked-eye Milky Way (peaks 9–11 PM in June, looking south/southeast).
  - [Mauna Kea stargazing program status](https://www.google.com/search?q=Mauna+Kea+Visitor+Information+Station+stargazing+program+2026) — check official site, free public programs have been intermittent.
- ⚠️ **Cold + altitude warnings**:
  - Temp at VIS drops to **40–55°F (4–13°C) after dark** even in summer — bring jackets/hats/long pants for everyone.
  - Altitude (9,200 ft / 2,800 m) — drink water, no alcohol, walk slow. Don't go to the summit without 4WD + acclimatization.
  - Drive down Saddle Road carefully at night; ~1.5 hr back to Kona.

### June 3 — Kona snorkel day + Black Sand Beach
- **[Two Step (Honaunau Bay)](https://www.google.com/search?q=Two+Step+Honaunau+Bay+snorkeling)** — best shore snorkeling, free. Get there by 8 AM for parking + calm water.
- **[Puʻuhonua o Hōnaunau](https://www.google.com/search?q=Puuhonua+o+Honaunau+National+Historical+Park)** (Place of Refuge) next door, $20/vehicle.
- Drive south on Hwy 11 to **[Punaluʻu Black Sand Beach](https://www.google.com/search?q=Punaluu+Black+Sand+Beach)** (~1.5 hr from Two Step) — sea turtles often resting on black sand.
- Return to Kona via Hwy 11 (~1.5 hr).

### June 4 — last day, late flight
- **[Hapuna Beach](https://www.google.com/search?q=Hapuna+Beach+State+Park)** (free, north of Kona ~45 min) — one of the best beaches in the US.
- Or chill / Costco run / **[Pololu Valley lookout](https://www.google.com/search?q=Pololu+Valley+Lookout)** at the far north tip if you want one more drive.
- Return rental by ~7 PM, fly 9:25 PM.
